Project

Design and build the website for ablute_, an intelligent sanitation platform.

The site must operate in two visual and conceptual modes:

Bathroom Mode

Health Mode

The two modes represent two different narratives of the same product.

• architecture
• product design
• water efficiency
• autonomous cleaning

Health Mode communicates:

• preventive health monitoring
• biomarker sensing
• medical analytics
• scientific credibility

The user must be able to switch modes via a mode toggle button in the header.

Scroll-Controlled Product Animation

When user scrolls down:

A product opening animation video plays.

The animation shows:

the equipment opening mechanically.

Important behavior:

Scroll down → video moves forward
Scroll up → video reverses
Stop scrolling → video pauses

This is scroll-synchronised playback.

Implementation concept:

scroll position = video time.

The image/video must:

blend with background
dark gradient edges
cinematic lighting

Scroll Transition

When the opening animation reaches the fully open state:

the page begins to scroll normally downward.

This creates the illusion that:

the user opened the device
and then enters the product details.
